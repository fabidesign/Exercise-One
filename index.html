The Nikon FE2 is an advanced semi-professional level, interchangeable-lens, 35 mm film, single lens reflex (SLR) camera. It was manufactured by Nippon Kogaku K. K. (Nikon Corporation since 1988) in Japan from 1983 to 1987 (available new from dealer stock until circa 1989).

Features

  • ASA/ISO range: ISO 12-4000
  • Shutter speed range: 8s-1/4000s;
  • Weight: 550g
  • Released: 1983 in Japan
